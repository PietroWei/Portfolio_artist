Who I am and how I create.

BIO

An Artist in the Digital Age

I am a visual artist and creative explorer using artificial intelligence as a brush and computation as a canvas. My passion is merging emerging technology with classical aesthetics to create works that challenge perception and tell stories of possible worlds.

Starting from a background in design and programming, I found in generative art a fertile ground where logic meets intuition. Each piece is the result of a dialogue between me and the machine, a process of continuous discovery and iteration.

My Process

My work begins with an idea, an emotion, or a concept. I use deep learning models like Stable Diffusion and Midjourney, writing detailed text prompts to guide the AI. This is just the beginning. Subsequently, I curate, edit, and combine the results, often using traditional photo editing software to achieve the final vision. It is a hybrid process that honors both code and the artistic eye.
